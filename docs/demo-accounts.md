## What Makes the Flipper Account Compelling

- **Portfolio view:** 3 projects at different stages (early, mid, complete) — shows Fierro's multi-project capability
- **Multi-user teams:** Different GCs per project, investor with read-only access across 2 projects
- **Approval workflow:** Marcus reviews contractor expenses — pending items, approved items, rejected duplicates
- **Investor visibility:** Diana sees costs across Elm St + Pine St without creating expenses — partner reporting
- **Budget health variance:** Some categories tight, some comfortable — creates visual interest
- **Pending vs included:** Active projects have pending expenses showing real workflow
- **Realistic flip economics:** $42K–$91K rehab budgets, contractor-grade materials (not luxury)
- **Vendor reuse across projects:** Metro HVAC, ProCoat Painters appear on multiple projects — shows vendor tracking value

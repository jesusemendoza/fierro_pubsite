# Pubsite Rewrite — Strategic Overview

## Why We're Doing This

### The Problem
The current getfierro.com markets to general contractors and builders. After an objective review of the site from four user perspectives (homeowner, flipper/investor, GC, builder), we found:

1. **GCs and builders won't convert** — The product is missing critical features they need (change orders, AIA billing, draw schedules, lien waivers, certified payroll). Marketing to them before these features exist wastes their time and damages our credibility.

2. **Homeowners and flippers could convert today** — The existing feature set (budget tracking, expense management, receipt capture, team collaboration, analytics) is exactly what these users need. But the site doesn't speak to them. The language is full of contractor jargon, and there's no pricing tier designed for homeowners.

3. **Zero social proof** — No testimonials, no screenshots, no demo video, no team page. For any user type, this is a dealbreaker. The fastest path to social proof is high-volume signups from homeowners and DIYers.

Full analysis: [User Type Analysis](../user-type-analysis/)

### The Strategy

**Phase 1: Win homeowners and flippers (this rewrite)**
- Rewrite all copy to speak directly to homeowners (primary) and flippers/investors (secondary)
- Add a $20/mo "Home" pricing tier between Free and Plus
- Add real product screenshots from a demo account
- Remove GC/builder-specific jargon and AI/MCP technical marketing
- Add audience-specific sections so homeowners and flippers see themselves immediately

**Phase 2: Stack social proof (post-launch)**
- Drive volume signups through the homeowner/DIY market (massive TAM, especially with the DIY renovation boom)
- Collect testimonials and usage data
- Build case studies around real homeowner and flipper projects

**Phase 3: Go after GCs (future)**
- Once we have social proof, reviews, and a proven product, expand features for GCs
- Add change order management, integrations, and contractor-specific workflows
- GCs will come to us because their homeowner clients are already using Fierro

### Why Homeowners First

- **Massive market** — DIY home improvement is a $600B+ industry. Every homeowner doing a renovation is a potential user.
- **Low friction** — Homeowners are less picky about enterprise features. They need simple, visual budget tracking. We have that.
- **Volume = social proof** — 1,000 homeowner signups creates more credibility than 10 GC signups
- **Viral loop** — Homeowners invite their contractors to Fierro. Contractors see value. Contractors bring their other projects. Bottom-up adoption.
- **Our features fit** — Budget tracking, expense logging, receipt capture, team sharing, analytics. This is exactly what a homeowner managing a renovation needs. We don't need to build anything new — we need to reframe what we have.

### What Changes

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| "Construction cost control" | "Track your renovation and project costs" |
| "For solo contractors getting started" | "For anyone tracking a project" |
| GC/builder jargon throughout | Homeowner/flipper language |
| 3 tiers (Free, Plus, Builder) | 4 tiers (Free, Home $20/mo, Plus $49/mo, Builder custom) |
| SVG illustrations | Real product screenshots |
| AI/MCP as a headline feature | AI as a background benefit, no jargon |
| Pain points aimed at PMs and field crews | Pain points aimed at homeowners and flippers |
| No audience segmentation | Dedicated section for homeowners, flippers, and investors |

### Success Criteria

A homeowner or flipper visiting getfierro.com should think:

> "Where has this been? Why am I not using this already?"

Anything less is a failure.

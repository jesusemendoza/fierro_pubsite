# Homeowner Perspective Analysis

> "I'm remodeling my kitchen / building an addition / managing a new build. I need to keep track of what I'm spending and make sure my contractor isn't ripping me off."

## Verdict: Would NOT use Fierro

## Trust Barriers

### No Social Proof
- Zero testimonials, reviews, or case studies anywhere on the site
- No "trusted by" logos or partner badges
- No user count or traction metrics
- A homeowner trusting their project finances to an unknown tool needs reassurance — there is none

### No Human Presence
- No team page, no founder story, no "About Us"
- No social media links (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram)
- No phone number — only a support email
- The company feels invisible; could be one person or a hundred

### No Product Visibility
- Zero screenshots of the actual product UI
- No demo video, walkthrough, or even animated GIFs
- Signing up is a complete leap of faith
- Homeowners especially need to see simplicity before committing

## Messaging Problems

### Language Isn't for Homeowners
- "Line-item budgets," "committed vs. actual costs," "vendor management" — this is contractor/PM jargon
- The schema description says "Built for general contractors, subcontractors, and project owners" — homeowners are an afterthought
- No section addresses the homeowner use case directly
- No language like "keep your contractor accountable" or "understand where your renovation dollars go"

### AI Messaging Is Confusing / Scary
- The "Why Fierro" page promotes ChatGPT and Claude reading project data
- For a non-technical homeowner, this sounds like a data privacy nightmare
- The Privacy Policy states: "Fierro Inc. is not responsible for data processing performed by third-party AI models"
- This liability shift is alarming for anyone who doesn't understand MCP/AI infrastructure

### No Outcome-Based Messaging
- Never says "average homeowner saves X%" or "catch overruns before they cost you thousands"
- All value props are abstract ("Real-Time Cost Visibility") rather than concrete ("See exactly how much your kitchen remodel is over budget")

## Feature Gaps for Homeowners

| Need | Status |
|------|--------|
| Simple, visual budget overview | Unknown — no screenshots |
| Compare contractor bids | Not mentioned |
| Track payments to contractor | Implied but not explicit |
| Photo documentation of progress | Not mentioned |
| Share view with spouse/partner | Team members exist, but framed for business |
| Milestone/draw tracking | Not mentioned |
| Contractor accountability features | Not mentioned |

## Pricing Concerns
- Free tier (1 project, 3 team members) is actually reasonable for a homeowner
- But there's no reason to believe the tool is simple enough for non-professionals
- "Community support" on Free tier — what community? No forum or community exists visibly

## What Would Fix This

1. Add a dedicated "For Homeowners" section or landing page
2. Show the product — screenshots of a kitchen remodel budget in progress
3. Add testimonials from real homeowners
4. Reframe features in homeowner language: "See where every dollar goes" not "Real-Time Cost Visibility"
5. Add a video walkthrough showing how simple it is
6. Remove or hide AI/MCP messaging for this audience
7. Add a phone number or chat widget for support
